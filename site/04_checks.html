Lifecycle Checks#

This section defines simple, automated completeness checks for the SIL 2 Emergency Stop thread. The goal is to detect missing lifecycle links early (e.g., requirements without verification).

Check 1: Every FSR must be verified#

Expected rule: Each Functional Safety Requirement (FSR) shall have at least one incoming verified_by link from a verification activity.
